Process of creating "What We Keep"

  1. Brainstormed about an object from our past and described it using the five senses and described how it changed our identity
  2. Once we completed our draft for the object from our past, we brainstormed an object from our present and described it and explain how you changed over time.
  3. For the object from our future, we filled out a prompt on what we wanted to be and live in the future, and thought of an item that could describe that. Then in the essay we would be describing our life in the future.

Reflection
