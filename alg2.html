Overview

The company has offered you a training to become more accustomed to building using the DESMOS program. The tasks that follow will ensue that you gain the necessary skills to succeed in designing the best box possible in preparation for Ship THIS! The annual company exposition!

Requirements For Box

  1. The box MUST NOT be a cube.
  2. The dimensions of the box MUST be so that the total volume is more than 24 cubic inches but less than 500 cubic inches.
  3. The dimensions(length, width, height) must be integer values.
  4. The design MUST be digitlized using DESMOS and animated so they can showcase their new design in an upcoming exposition
